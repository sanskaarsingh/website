THE ARTIST

Sanskaar Singh is a rapper, storyteller, and self-taught music producer who refuses to be boxed in. Known for crafting dark, cinematic experiences through sound, he writes, composes, and produces every aspect of his work, from A to Z. His music isn’t just heard; it’s felt, a reflection of internal chaos, identity, and transformation.

He despises being compared to others, constantly pushing boundaries to find a sound that’s entirely his own. With an obsession for storytelling and a love for visual worlds, his project AODS is a haunting testament to his disturbing yet immersive style. Currently, he’s working on Kaali, an ambitious continuation of his evolution as an artist who believes that change isn’t optional, it’s law.

ABOUT CRYSISCULTURE

CrysisCulture is a movement. A rebellion against creative gatekeeping. A home for the unheard. A vision built #FORTHECAUSE. Born out of the struggles of underground artists, CrysisCulture exists to uplift raw talent and give them a stage they’ve been denied for too long. We provide full-scale creative support, from music video production, beat making, mixing & mastering, distribution, artist management, to poster and publicity design, not to profit, but to empower.

We don’t charge. We collaborate. If you’ve got the hunger, we’ve got the lens.

Artists who can’t afford high-budget visuals or access to proper platforms can simply reach out to us, and if their voice speaks truth, we feature them, shoot for them, build with them. No questions asked. Just honest art.

CrysisCulture thrives on originality. From short films to music videos, documentaries to experimental visuals, we chase ideas that are bold, cinematic, uncomfortable and most importantly, authentic. This is for the misfits, the overlooked, the ones told they’re “too different.” This is for the storytellers, the visionaries, and the broken hearts turned into art.

We’re not just making content. We’re creating culture. CrysisCulture.
