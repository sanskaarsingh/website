THE ARTIST

Sanskaar Singh is a rapper, storyteller, and self-taught music producer who refuses to be boxed in. Known for crafting dark, cinematic experiences through sound, he writes, composes, and produces every aspect of his work, from A to Z. His music isn’t just heard; it’s felt, a reflection of internal chaos, identity, and transformation.

He despises being compared to others, constantly pushing boundaries to find a sound that’s entirely his own. With an obsession for storytelling and a love for visual worlds, his project AODS is a haunting testament to his disturbing yet immersive style. Currently, he’s working on Kaali, an ambitious continuation of his evolution as an artist who believes that change isn’t optional, it’s law.
